INSTRUCTIONS:


There are two players able to play this game
Player One: all Capital pieces
Player Two: all lowercase pieces

Commands:
move [starting place] [destination] e.g. [move 8 12] will move piece at index 8 to index 12 as long as its an adjecent tile

upgrade [upgrade type] [tile with piece on it]  e.g. [upgrade pbds 30 ] will upgrade piece at tile 30 to a pbds piece 

saw [tile with LESS piece in it] e.g. [saw 9] will make piece at tile 9 give one piece of wood as long as its a less piece and its in a forst (#)

dig [tile with PBDS piece in it] e.g. [dig 11] will make piece at tile 11 give one fule as long as its a pbds and its in a peat bog (~)

spawn [tile where it will spawn] e.g. [spawn 8] will make the baron spawn a serf in tile 8 (only if its adjacent) 
(only barons can spawn pieces onto the board)


Destroying pieces:
surround an opposing piece with two of your own pieces to destroy it and gain points

Win Condition:
try and make two of your pieces surround the opposing players baron to kill it and win the game
